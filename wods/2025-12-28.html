Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds for Quality:
  • 250m Row
  • 10 Good Mornings (empty bar)
  • 10 Hanging Knee Raises
  • 5 Inchworms with Push-Up
  • 10 Reverse Lunges (bodyweight)

Primer: Finish with 2 rounds of 3 High Pull + 3 Hang Power Cleans (light barbell) to prime your pulls.

Part B — Strength

  • Power Clean + Clean Pull Complex
  • Every 2:00 for 6 Rounds:
  • 1 Power Clean @ 205 lb (~77%)
  • 2 Clean Pulls @ 225 lb (~85%)

Goal: Smooth bar path, violent extension. Focus on posture and drive from the floor.

Part C — MetCon

  • “The Grinder” — 18 Minute Cap
  • For Time:
  • 20 Power Cleans (155/105)
  • 30 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 40 Wall Balls (20/14)
  • 800m Run
  • 40 Wall Balls (20/14)
  • 30 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 20 Power Cleans (155/105)

Stimulus: Moderate-heavy barbell cycling and high-volume midline demand.

Goal: 14–17 minutes — smooth transitions and consistent pacing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4 Rounds:
  • 12 Romanian Deadlifts (135 lb, slow tempo)
  • 10 Ring Dips
  • 15 Back Extensions
  • 20 Banded Hamstring Curls
  • 1:00 Plank Hold

Accessory Intent: Posterior chain endurance and midline control.

Finish with 10–15 minutes of posterior chain mobility (hamstrings, lats, glutes).
