Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds, steady pace:
  • 200 m Run
  • 10 Empty Bar Good Mornings
  • 10 Sit-Ups
  • 8 Burpees
  • 6 Barbell Rows (light)
  • 5 Air Squats

Goal: Raise heart rate and prime posterior chain + midline for barbell rows, GHDs, and burpees.

Part B — Strength

  • Barbell Row: 5×8 @ ~60–65% of MetCon load (≈60 lbs)
  • Rest 60–90 seconds between sets.
  • Then: 3×10 Glute Bridge Hold w/ Barbell (3-second pause each rep)

Focus: Controlled pull, solid hinge position.

Intent: Prime posterior chain and reinforce pulling mechanics.

Part C — MetCon

  • For Time @ High Effort
  • 10-20-30-40
  • Echo Bike Calories
  • GHD Sit-Ups
  • Lateral Burpees Over Bar
  • 2-4-6-8 Barbell Rows (95 lbs)

Stimulus: High-effort cardio with strong posterior and core bias.

Goal: Finish in 12–16 minutes.

Strategy: Smooth on bike early, unbroken GHDs if possible, consistent burpees, and clean rows.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4 Sets:
  • 12 Dumbbell Bench Press (50/35s)
  • 15 Reverse Hypers or Banded Good Mornings
  • 20 Weighted Sit-Ups (25 lb plate)
  • Then: 10–15 min Easy Echo Bike Cooldown (Zone 2)
  • 5–10 min Hip & Hamstring Mobility

Intent: Build upper-body volume and posterior chain endurance while promoting recovery.
