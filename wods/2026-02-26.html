Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 30s Row / Bike / Ski
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 10 Light Wall Balls
  • 5 Box Step-Overs (each side)

Part B — Strength

  • Front Squat: Build to 5 reps @ 240 lbs

Focus: Upright torso, controlled descent, strong drive out of the hole.

Intent: Prime the legs without excessive fatigue before the Open effort.

Part C — MetCon

  • CrossFit Open 26.1 — 12 Minute Time Cap:
  • 20 Wall Balls
  • 18 Box Jump-Overs
  • 30 Wall Balls
  • 18 Box Jump-Overs
  • 40 Wall Balls
  • 18 Box Step-Overs (Holding Wall Ball)
  • 66 Wall Balls
  • 18 Box Step-Overs (Holding Wall Ball)
  • 40 Wall Balls
  • 18 Box Jump-Overs
  • 30 Wall Balls
  • 18 Box Jump-Overs
  • 20 Wall Balls

Standards: 20/14 lb ball (10'/9'), 24"/20" box. Wall ball must be held during designated step-over sets.

Stimulus: High-volume leg stamina with grip and midline fatigue under pressure.

Strategy: Break wall balls early (10–15 reps). Control breathing before the 66. Stay steady and unbroken on wall-ball step-overs if possible.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 5×8 Dumbbell Push Press (50/35)
  • 3×12 Banded Pull-Aparts
  • 4×12 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 10–15 Minutes Mobility (hips, ankles, shoulders)

Accessory Intent: Shoulder endurance, midline stability, and recovery support post-Open effort.
