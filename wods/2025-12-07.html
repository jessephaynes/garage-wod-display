Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds, smooth and steady flow:
  • 200 m Easy Row
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs
  • 10 Lunges (bodyweight)
  • 10 Scap Push-Ups
  • 10 Banded Good Mornings
  • 10 GHD or AbMat Sit-Ups

Focus: Open hips, spine, and shoulders.

Goal: Light sweat before moving into the long flush.

Part B — Aerobic Flush

  • 5 Rounds:
  • 2 min Row
  • 2 min Ski
  • 2 min Echo Bike
  • 2 min Rest

Stimulus: Zone 2–3 conversational pace; no sprints.

Goal: HR ~120–140 bpm, smooth cadence and breathing.

Part C — Core + Stability

  • 3 Rounds (not for time):
  • 15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 10/side Single-Arm DB Bench Press (50/35)
  • 15 DB RDLs (50/35s)
  • 30 sec Side Plank (per side)

Focus: Core control and posture reinforcement.

Part D — Accessory & Mobility (Optional)

  • A. Shoulder & Scap Flow
  • 3×15 Banded Face Pulls
  • 3×15 Banded External Rotations
  • 3×30 sec Ring Support Hold
  • B. Posterior Chain Mobility
  • 3×60 sec Couch Stretch (each leg)
  • 2×60 sec Pigeon Stretch
  • 2×1 min Bar Hang
  • C. Breathing & Reset
  • 5 min Easy Nasal-Breathing Walk or Bike
  • Optional: 10 min Foam Roll or Cold Shower

Intent: Recovery, movement quality, and relaxation.
