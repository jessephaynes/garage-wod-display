Today's WOD — Iron Legs (Rain Edition)

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Echo Rower (easy)
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 10 Glute Bridges
  • 10 Alternating Reverse Lunges
  • 20s Hollow Hold
  • 10 PVC Good Mornings

Part B — Strength

  • Back Squat: Build to a heavy 5-rep set, then 3×5 @ 80% of top set
  • Reference 1RM: 360 lb → Working range ~275–290 lb

Focus: Controlled descent, upright torso, powerful drive up.

Intent: Lower-body strength under control — no rushing.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Wheelhouse – Rain Edition”
  • 18-Minute AMRAP:
  • 20/15 Cal Echo Bike (or 250 m Row)
  • 20 Wall Balls (20/14 lb)
  • 15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 10 Sandbag Squats (100/80 lb)

Stimulus: Aerobic grind; legs and lungs on fire, grip stays fresh.

Goal: 4–6 rounds with steady pacing on the bike or rower.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3–4 Rounds (not for time):
  • 12 Bulgarian Split Squats per leg (50/35 lb optional)
  • 15 Hip Extensions or Banded Good Mornings
  • 30s Side Plank per side
  • 12 Bench Leg Raises
  • 2 min easy Echo Bike recovery

Accessory Intent: Unilateral leg work, glute/hamstring reinforcement, and core endurance — no grip fatigue.
