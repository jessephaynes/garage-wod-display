Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 300 m Row
  • 10 Box Step-Overs
  • 10 Kip Swings
  • 12 Alternating Reverse Lunges
  • 30 Single Unders
  • Then 2 Rounds:
  • 5 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 5 Strict Pull-Ups
  • 20 Double Unders

Part B — Strength

  • Weighted Pull-Ups: Every 3 min x 5 sets (5–8 reps)
  • 10 Hollow Rocks after each set

Focus: Strict strength, full range of motion, controlled reps.

Intent: Build pulling capacity and durability for high-rep fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • For Time:
  • 21-15-9-15-21
  • GHD Sit-Ups
  • Box Jump Overs (24/20)
  • Double Unders (x3 reps)
  • Pull-Ups

Stimulus: High-skill fatigue management with heavy midline and shoulder demand.

Goal: 14–20 minutes. Stay composed and avoid early burnout on double unders.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×12 GHD Sit-Ups (tempo)
  • 3×30 sec L-Sit Hold
  • 4×50 Single Unders (controlled)
  • 3×15 Calf Raises
  • 3×15 Banded Lat Pulldowns
  • 3×12 Banded Face Pulls
  • 2×1 min Dead Hang
  • 10–15 min easy Bike or Row

Accessory Intent: Build midline resilience, jumping durability, and shoulder health.
