Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Row
  • 10 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 10 Empty Bar Bent-Over Rows
  • 8 Bar-Facing Step-Overs
  • 10 Banded Face Pulls
  • Then:
  • 2 x 20 sec Hard Effort on Erg
  • 40 sec Easy Spin Between Efforts

Part B — Strength

  • Barbell Bent-Over Row
  • 8 reps @ 155 lbs
  • 8 reps @ 185 lbs
  • 6 reps @ 205 lbs
  • 6 reps @ 225 lbs
  • 6+ reps @ 225 lbs (leave 1 rep in tank)
  • Superset Each Set With:
  • 10–12 Ring Rows (2-sec pause at top)

Focus: Torso just above parallel, strict pull, controlled eccentric.

Intent: Build upper back strength and positional control without jerking from the hips.

Part C — MetCon

  • 18-Minute AMRAP:
  • 12 Bar-Facing Burpees
  • 15/12 Calorie Erg (Row, Echo Bike, or Ski)
  • 12 Ring Rows
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Sustained grind — upper body fatigue meets engine control.

Goal: 5–7 rounds with consistent pacing and smooth transitions.

Pacing: First 5 minutes at 80%, middle steady and uncomfortable, final 5 minutes push the burpees.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×12 Single-Arm DB Rows (50 lb)
  • 3×15 Banded Lat Pulldowns
  • 3×20 Banded Rear Delt Flys
  • 4×12 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 3×20 Alternating Dead Bugs
  • 30–40 min Zone 2 Erg (Nasal Breathing)

Accessory Intent: Add upper-back volume, reinforce posterior chain, and build aerobic base without high intensity.
