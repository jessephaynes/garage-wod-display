Today's WOD — Iron Lung

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds (easy pace):
  • 10 Burpees (step up/down)
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 200 m Easy Echo Bike
  • 10 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 5 Box Step-Ups (24/20”)

Goal: Open hips, core, and shoulders. Gradually ramp heart rate to working level.

Part B — MetCon

  • “Iron Lung” — 30-Minute AMRAP
  • 12 Burpee Box Jump Overs (24/20”)
  • 15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 20/16 Cal Echo Bike
  • 12 Dumbbell Front-Rack Lunges (50/35s, 6/leg)

Stimulus: Aerobic endurance meets core control — legs and lungs will be the limiter.

Pacing: Smooth and sustainable — aim for 4–5+ rounds. Manage transitions, avoid redlining early.

Tip: Step down on box jumps if needed; stay efficient and quiet.

Part C — Optional Finisher / Recovery

  • 3 Rounds (quality):
  • 15 Banded Good Mornings
  • 1:00 Easy Echo Bike Flush
  • 20 Banded Face Pulls
  • 1:00 Couch Stretch per leg

Intent: Restore hamstrings, flush quads, and cool the system post-WOD.
