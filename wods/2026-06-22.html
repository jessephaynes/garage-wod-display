Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds
  • 250m easy Row
  • 10 band pull-aparts
  • 10 PVC pass-throughs
  • 10 empty bar strict presses
  • 10 push-ups
  • 20-second front rack stretch
  • 20-second overhead hold
  • 2 x 5 Strict Press building to first work weight

Part B — Strength

  • Strict Press
  • 5 sets x 5 reps
  • 115 lb
  • 125 lb
  • 135 lb
  • 145 lb
  • 155–165 lb
  • Optional heavy set of 3

Focus: Midline stability, vertical bar path, and consistent lockout.

Intent: Build pressing strength without grinding. Every rep should look the same.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5,000m Row For Time

Stimulus: Long aerobic test with a strong mental component.

Goal: Sustainable hard effort with a negative split over the final 1,000m.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3–5 rounds
  • 12 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 15 Ring Rows with 3-second negative
  • 20 Band Face Pulls
  • 1:00 Front Rack Stretch

Accessory Intent: Build upper-back endurance and posterior-chain strength to support pressing and rowing posture.
