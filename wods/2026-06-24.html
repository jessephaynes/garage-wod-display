Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds: 250m Row
  • 5 Inchworms
  • 10 Empty Bar Muscle Cleans
  • 5 Front Squats
  • 5 Push Press
  • 10 Hollow Rocks
  • 3 x 3 Tall Cleans
  • 3 x 2 Clean + Front Squat + Jerk @ light weight

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:00 x 8 Sets
  • 1 Clean
  • 1 Hang Clean
  • 1 Jerk
  • Build from 135 to 225–235 lb

Focus: Fast elbows, patient off the floor, aggressive turnover.

Intent: Heavy enough to demand precision, not so heavy that technique deteriorates.

Part C — MetCon

  • For Time
  • 30-24-18-12-6 Echo Bike Calories
  • 30-24-18-12-6 Toes-to-Bar
  • After each round: 3 Clean & Jerks @ 185 lb
  • 800m Run

Stimulus: Aerobic ladder with gymnastic fatigue and recurring moderate-heavy barbell work.

Goal: Finish in 18–24 minutes while maintaining crisp barbell mechanics.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Sets: 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 12 Strict Toes-to-Rings
  • 20 Banded Face Pulls
  • 1:00 Front Rack Stretch

Accessory Intent: Build midline stamina, posterior-chain durability, and front-rack mobility.
