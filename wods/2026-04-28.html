Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Row
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 8 Inchworms (with push-up)
  • 10 Front Rack Stretch (empty bar)
  • Then 2 Rounds:
  • 5 Front Squats (light)
  • 5 Hang Power Cleans (light)

Part B — Strength

  • Front Squat: Build to Heavy 5
  • Target: 225–255 lbs
  • Then 1×8 @ 185 lbs

Focus: Upright torso, elbows high, smooth tempo.

Intent: Prime legs without excessive fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • 3 Rounds for Time:
  • 30 Cal Row
  • 20 Front Squats (95/115 lbs)
  • 10 Power Cleans (95/115 lbs)

Stimulus: Fast, leg-heavy conditioning with a light, cycleable barbell.

Strategy: Row at 80–85%. Front squats unbroken. Power cleans quick singles or touch-and-go early.

Goal: 12–15 minutes with minimal breaks.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×10/leg DB Bulgarian Split Squats (50/35)
  • 4×15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 3×1:00 Front Plank
  • 5 Rounds:
  • 12/10 Cal Echo Bike
  • Rest 1:1
  • Mobility:
  • Banded Front Rack Stretch – 2 min
  • Couch Stretch – 2 min/side

Accessory Intent: Build single-leg strength, midline endurance, and flush the legs.
