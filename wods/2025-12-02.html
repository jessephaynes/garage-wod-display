Today's WOD — December 2, 2025

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 200 m Run
  • 10 Jumping Air Squats
  • 10 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 10 Empty Bar Good Mornings
  • 5 Hang Power Cleans (light barbell)

Prep Focus: Open hips, prime posterior chain, groove clean mechanics.

Part B — Strength

  • Hang Power Clean + Front Squat Complex
  • 1 Hang Power Clean + 1 Front Squat × 6 sets
  • Build to a heavy complex for the day (target 215–235 lb)
  • Rest 2:00 between sets

Goal: Crisp bar speed and stable catch — no grinding.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Hang Tough” — 18-Minute AMRAP
  • 12 Hang Power Cleans (135/95)
  • 15 Toes-to-Bar
  • 18 Wall Balls (20/14)
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Grip and midline challenge. Manage cleans in 2 sets and control transitions.

Goal: 4–5 rounds with steady pacing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×12 Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts (50/35s)
  • 3×15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 3×15 Hip Extensions (GHD or bands)
  • Finisher: 3× max plank hold (:45–1:30)

Intent: Reinforce hinge pattern and build midline endurance.
