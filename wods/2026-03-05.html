Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 200 m Row
  • 10 Banded Face Pulls
  • 10 Push-Ups
  • 12 Air Squats
  • 10 Hollow Rocks
  • 20 sec Echo Bike build
  • Then 2 Rounds:
  • 6 Strict Pull-Ups
  • 8 Dumbbell Push Press (35s)
  • 6 Box Step-Ups each leg

Part B — Strength

  • Bench Press: 5×5 @ 205 lb
  • After each set: 8 Ring Rows + 12 Banded Pull-Aparts

Focus: Controlled descent and explosive press.

Intent: Build pressing strength while balancing shoulders with pulling volume.

Part C — MetCon

  • EMOM 20 (:35 work / :25 rest)
  • Odd Minutes: Echo Bike
  • Even Minutes: Row
  • Immediately into:
  • 18-Minute AMRAP
  • 15 Cal Echo Bike
  • 15 Cal Row

Stimulus: Aerobic engine and pacing discipline.

Goal: Maintain consistent calorie output in the EMOM and hit 6–8 rounds in the AMRAP.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×12 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 4×12 Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts (50s)
  • 3 Rounds:
  • 20 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 1:00 Hollow Hold
  • 30 sec Side Plank each side
  • Optional Flush: 10 min easy Echo Bike or Row

Accessory Intent: Posterior chain durability, core capacity, and aerobic recovery.
