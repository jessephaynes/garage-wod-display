Today's WOD — Press and Push

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 300 m Echo Row
  • 10 Scap Push-Ups
  • 10 Banded Pull-Aparts
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs
  • 5 Push-Ups to Down Dog
  • 5 Empty Bar Bench Press

Intent: Prep shoulders, chest, and triceps while getting heart rate up.

Part B — Strength

  • Bench Press: 5×3 @ 80% (230 lbs)
  • Then 1×AMRAP @ 70% (205 lbs), stop 1–2 reps shy of failure

Focus: Tight setup, strong leg drive, controlled descent.

Intent: Build upper body pressing strength.

Part C — MetCon

  • For Time (18 min cap):
  • 3 Rounds:
  • 15 Dumbbell Push Press (50/35)
  • 20 Box Jump Overs (24/20)
  • 25 GHD Sit-Ups or AbMat Sit-Ups
  • 400 m Run

Stimulus: Moderate-high intensity with a strong push-pull balance. Shoulders and lungs will burn.

Goal: 12–15 minutes with smooth transitions and consistent pacing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×10 Dumbbell Row (per arm, heavy)
  • 3×15 Banded Face Pulls
  • 4×12 Dumbbell Lateral Raises
  • 3×20 Banded Triceps Pushdowns
  • 10 min Easy Echo Bike Flush

Intent: Shoulder health, upper back balance, and triceps volume to support pressing strength.
