Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds: 250m Row, 10 PVC/band pass-throughs, 10 Air Squats, 8 Scap Pull-Ups, 5 Inchworms
  • Then 2 rounds: 5 Muscle Snatch (empty bar), 5 Overhead Squat, 5 Snatch Balance, 3 Tall Snatch

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:00 x 8 sets: 1 Power Snatch + 1 Hang Squat Snatch + 1 Overhead Squat
  • Build gradually but cap at 135–155 lb

Focus: Crisp turnover, aggressive extension, stable receiving position.

Intent: Get quality reps under the bar without chasing heavy singles. Today should feel like sharpening the blade, not testing it.

Part C — MetCon

  • 4 Rounds For Time:
  • 15/12 Cal Echo Bike
  • 12 Dumbbell Snatches (50/35, alternating)
  • 9 Burpee Box Jump Overs (24")
  • 6 Bar-Facing Burpees

Stimulus: Spicy, breathing hard from the opening bike and never fully recovering.

Goal: 10–14 minutes.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds: 15 GHD Hip Extensions, 12 Strict Toes-to-Bar or Hanging Leg Raises, 1:00 Front Rack Stretch

Accessory Intent: Restore overhead and front rack positions while getting some trunk work after the snatching.
