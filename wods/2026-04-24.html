Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250m Row (build each round)
  • 10 Alternating Reverse Lunges
  • 10 Banded Good Mornings
  • 30 Single Unders
  • 5 Sandbag Deadlifts
  • Then 2 Sets:
  • 5 Sandbag Cleans (build)
  • 30 Double Unders

Part B — Strength

  • Deadlift Complex: Every 3:00 x 5 sets
  • 3 Romanian Deadlifts
  • 2 Paused Deadlifts (below knee)
  • Load: 225–275 lbs

Focus: Hamstring tension, strong lat engagement, controlled tempo.

Intent: Build posterior chain strength without excessive fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • 3 Rounds For Time:
  • 800m Row
  • 10 Sandbag Cleans (150#)
  • 100 Double Unders

Stimulus: Long aerobic grind with accumulating fatigue.

Goal: 20–28 minutes with consistent pacing.

Pacing: Row at 75–80%, smooth sandbag reps, relaxed rope rhythm.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 3×20 Banded Hip Extensions
  • 4×12 DB Walking Lunges (50/35)
  • 4×8–12 Strict Pull-Ups
  • Mobility: Couch Stretch + Hamstrings + Calves

Accessory Intent: Build midline endurance, unilateral leg strength, and restore mobility post-conditioning.
