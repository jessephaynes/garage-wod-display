Today's WOD — Walk the Line

Walk the Line

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 200m Row
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs
  • 10 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 10 Kip Swings
  • 8 Wall Walks (controlled)
  • 5 Empty Bar Strict Press
  • 30s Handstand Hold

Focus: Open shoulders, activate posterior chain and core for inversion and pulling movements.

Part B — Strength

  • Push Press: Build to a heavy 5-rep
  • Then 2×5 @ 90% of top set
  • Accessory Superset (3 rounds):
  • 8 Strict Chest-to-Bar Pull-Ups
  • 10 Front Rack Lunges (light)

Intent: Reinforce overhead mechanics before the MetCon and prep upper pulling strength.

Part C — MetCon

  • 6 Sets:
  • 30ft Handstand Walk
  • 4 Bar Muscle-Ups (or 6 Chest-to-Bar Pull-Ups)
  • 5 Shoulder-to-Overhead (155/105 lb)
  • 6 Calorie Row
  • 7 Burpee Pull-Ups
  • Rest 1:1 between sets

Stimulus: Short, intense sets — 90–120 seconds of work per round. Prioritize unbroken movements and crisp transitions.

Goal: Maintain consistent pacing across all 6 rounds at ~80–85% effort.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 10 Ring Face Pulls
  • 10 DB External Rotations (per arm)
  • 15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 1:00 Weighted Plank Hold (45/25 plate)
  • Then:
  • 3×10 DB Lateral Raises (light)
  • 3×12 Banded Tricep Pushdowns

Accessory Intent: Shoulder health, midline reinforcement, and pressing balance.
