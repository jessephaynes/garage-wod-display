Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds: 250m Row, 10 Air Squats, 8 Ring Rows, 6 Push-Ups, 5 Front Squats with empty bar and 2-second pause.
  • Then 2 sets: 5 Empty-Bar Front Squats, 5 Empty-Bar Push Press, 5 Empty-Bar Front Rack Reverse Lunges.

Part B — Strength

  • Front Squat: Build to a heavy set of 5 reps.
  • Then 3 x 5 @ 255 lb.
  • Rest 2–3 minutes between working sets.

Focus: Bracing, upright torso, and aggressive drive out of the hole.

Intent: Develop absolute leg strength while reinforcing efficient front rack positioning under fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5 Rounds For Time:
  • 15/12 Cal Echo Bike
  • 10 Front Rack Lunges (135/95 lb)
  • 8 Chest-to-Bar Pull-Ups
  • 6 Burpee Box Jump Overs (24 in)

Stimulus: Mid-length grinder with leg fatigue accumulating every round.

Goal: Finish in 14–18 minutes while maintaining consistent pacing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 rounds: 15 GHD Hip Extensions, 20 Banded Hamstring Curls, 20 GHD Sit-Ups, 1:00 Front Rack Stretch.

Accessory Intent: Build durability in the posterior chain and trunk while improving front rack mobility.
