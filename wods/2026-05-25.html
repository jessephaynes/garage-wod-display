Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds, smooth pace:
  • 250m Echo Row
  • 10 alternating dumbbell hang clean + push press (35s)
  • 10 box step-ups
  • 8 ring rows
  • 20 single-unders or easy double-under practice
  • Then: 2 x 20 sec hard / 40 sec easy on SkiErg

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:00 x 6 rounds:
  • Minute 1: 10/8 Echo Bike calories + 6 burpees
  • Minute 2: 8 dumbbell front squats (2x35) + 8 V-ups or GHD sit-ups

Focus: Smooth breathing and efficient transitions.

Intent: Prime the aerobic system without accumulating fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • AMRAP 16:
  • 400m run
  • 15 wall-facing shoulder taps
  • 12 dumbbell hang power cleans (2x35)
  • 10/8 SkiErg calories

Stimulus: Steady aerobic conditioning with light muscular fatigue in the shoulders and grip.

Goal: Continuous movement for 16 minutes with sustainable pacing and a stronger final push.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 5 min easy Echo Bike
  • 5–10 min mobility:
  • Couch stretch
  • Lat stretch on rig
  • Pigeon pose
  • Thoracic opener on bench or foam roller

Accessory Intent: Flush the legs, open the hips and shoulders, and finish recovered instead of depleted.
