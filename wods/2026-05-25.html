Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds: 250m Row, 10 PVC pass-throughs, 8 Cossack squats, 10 alternating light DB hang clean + push press, 5 burpees, 20 sec hollow hold
  • Then 2 rounds: 5 tall cleans, 5 front squats, 5 push jerks with empty barbell

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:30 x 6 sets:
  • 1 Power Clean
  • 1 Hang Squat Clean
  • 2 Front Squats
  • Build across sets
  • Suggested loading: 155–175, 185–205, finishing 215–235 if moving well

Focus: Fast elbows, vertical dip and drive, and stable front rack positioning.

Intent: Build moderate-heavy loading with clean movement quality and no missed reps.

Part C — MetCon

  • 4 Rounds for Time:
  • 400m Run
  • 15 Front Squats (135/95)
  • 12 Box Jump Overs (24/20)
  • 9 Toes-to-Bar

Stimulus: High breathing demand with accumulating leg fatigue and sustained movement under pressure.

Goal: Finish in 12–16 minutes while keeping front squats controlled and efficient.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3–4 rounds:
  • 15 GHD hip extensions
  • 12 single-arm DB rows each side (50 lb)
  • 20 banded face pulls
  • 40 sec side plank each side
  • Rest 60–90 sec between rounds

Accessory Intent: Reinforce posterior chain strength, upper-back balance, and trunk stability after front rack volume and running fatigue.
