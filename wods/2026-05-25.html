Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds: 250m Row, 10 PVC pass-throughs or banded shoulder openers, 8 Empty Bar Muscle Cleans, 8 Front Squats, 6 Push Press, 6 Box Step-Ups (24"), 30 sec Hollow Hold
  • Then 2 rounds: 3 Tall Cleans, 3 Hang Power Cleans, 3 Front Squats

Part B — Strength

  • Every 3:00 x 6 Sets:
  • 2 Power Cleans + 1 Split Jerk
  • Suggested Loading: 155, 175, 195, 205, 215, 225–235+

Focus: Violent extension, efficient turnover, and aggressive footwork under the bar.

Intent: Build to a heavy, technically sharp complex without turning the session into a max-out grind.

Part C — MetCon

  • 4 Rounds For Time:
  • 500m Row
  • 12 Front Rack Walking Lunges (50s or 95 lb)
  • 9 Burpee Box Jump Overs (24")
  • 6 Shoulder-to-Overhead (155/105)

Stimulus: Sustained aerobic pressure with leg fatigue and demanding barbell cycling.

Goal: Finish in 12–16 minutes while maintaining steady movement and clean transitions.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Sets:
  • 15 Ring Rows (tempo 31X1)
  • 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 20 Banded Face Pulls
  • 1:00 Farmer Hold (50 lb DBs)
  • Rest 60–90 sec between sets

Accessory Intent: Improve upper-back endurance and trunk stability for stronger Olympic positions and healthier shoulders.
