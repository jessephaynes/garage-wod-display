Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Ski Erg
  • 10 Alternating Spiderman Lunges
  • 12 Empty Bar Romanian Deadlifts
  • 10 Ring Rows
  • 8 Box Step-Ups (24”)
  • Then:
  • 2 x 20-Second Handstand Hold or Wall Walk Practice

Warm-Up Focus: Prime posterior chain, shoulders, and midline while elevating heart rate gradually for pulling volume and hinging mechanics.

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:00 x 8 Sets
  • 1 Clean Pull
  • 1 Hang Power Clean
  • 1 Front Squat
  • Sets 1–3 Build
  • Sets 4–8 @ 205–225 lbs

Focus: Vertical extension on the pull, aggressive turnover, and strong front squat positioning.

Intent: Build explosive clean mechanics without creating excessive fatigue before conditioning.

Part C — MetCon

  • 16-Minute AMRAP:
  • 12/10 Cal Echo Bike
  • 10 Toes-to-Bar
  • 8 Dumbbell Front Rack Walking Lunges (50s/35s)
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Moderately aggressive engine work with core fatigue and cyclical recovery.

Goal: 4–6 rounds with smooth transitions and sustainable pacing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×12 Banded Face Pulls
  • 4×12 Dumbbell Bent-Over Reverse Flys
  • 4×30-Second Ring Support Hold
  • 4×15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 4×12 Single-Leg Dumbbell RDLs Each Side
  • 4×20 Banded Hamstring Curls
  • 20-Minute Easy Row, Bike, or Conversational Run
  • Mobility Cooldown:
  • 1-Minute Couch Stretch Each Side
  • 1-Minute Lat Stretch on Rig
  • 1-Minute Pigeon Pose Each Side
  • 20 Banded Shoulder Pass-Throughs

Accessory Intent: Build upper back stability, posterior chain durability, and aerobic recovery capacity.
