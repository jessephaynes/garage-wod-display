Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Ski Erg
  • 10 Alternating Samson Lunges
  • 10 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 8 Dumbbell Deadlifts (50/35)
  • 20 Single-Unders
  • Then:
  • 2 x 20-Second Handstand Hold Against Wall
  • 5 Empty Bar Muscle Cleans + Front Squats

Warm-Up Intent: Prep shoulders and posterior chain for front rack cycling, pulling stamina, and inverted pressing.

Part B — Strength

  • Front Squat + Split Jerk Complex
  • Every 3 Minutes x 6 Sets:
  • 2 Front Squats + 1 Split Jerk
  • Sets 1–2: 185 lbs
  • Sets 3–4: 205 lbs
  • Sets 5–6: 225–245 lbs

Focus: Vertical torso in squat and aggressive leg drive into the jerk.

Stimulus: Heavy but crisp barbell cycling under moderate fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • 18-Minute AMRAP:
  • 12 Toes-to-Bar
  • 9 Front Rack Walking Lunges (135/95)
  • 12 Handstand Push-Ups
  • 250 m Row

Stimulus: Upper-body stamina and midline fatigue paired with breathing interference from the row.

Goal: 4–6 rounds with smooth transitions and sustainable pacing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4 Rounds:
  • 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 12 Tempo Romanian Deadlifts (135)
  • 1:00 Banded Hamstring Stretch
  • 3 Sets:
  • 12 Dumbbell Z-Press (35s)
  • 15 Ring Face Pulls
  • 20 Banded External Rotations
  • 20-Minute Easy Sustain:
  • 500 m Echo Bike
  • 400 m Jog
  • 250 m Ski Erg

Accessory Intent: Posterior chain durability, shoulder stability, and aerobic recovery work.
