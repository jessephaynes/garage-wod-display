Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Row
  • 10 Alternating DB Lunges (35s)
  • 10 Ring Rows
  • 30 sec Jump Rope
  • Then:
  • 2 x 100m Build-Up Runs

Part B — Strength

  • Front Squat + Bike: Every 3:00 x 5
  • 8 Front Squats @ 185 lbs
  • 10/8 Cal Echo Bike

Focus: Controlled breathing under load.

Intent: Finish early and recover each round—no redlining.

Part C — MetCon

  • 20-Minute AMRAP:
  • 500 m Ski Erg
  • 15 Barbell Hang Power Cleans (95 lbs)
  • 20/15 Cal Row
  • 15 Toes-to-Bar

Stimulus: Sustainable aerobic effort—full-body engine.

Goal: 4–6 rounds with consistent pacing.

Pacing: Smooth Ski and Row (~70–75%), unbroken barbell, controlled T2B sets.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • Option 1: 30 min Zone 2 Bike or Row
  • Option 2: 5 Rounds
  • 3 min easy cardio
  • 1 min moderate effort

Accessory Intent: Build aerobic base and promote recovery.
