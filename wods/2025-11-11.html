Today's WOD — Steam Engine

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds, smooth and steady:
  • 200 m Echo Row
  • 10 Good Mornings (empty barbell)
  • 8 Front Rack Lunges (barbell)
  • 10 PVC Snatch Grip Pass-Throughs
  • 5 Burpees

Focus: Hip mobility, core stability, and gradual heart rate ramp-up.

Part B — Strength

  • Power Clean + Front Squat + Jerk Complex
  • 5 Sets @ moderate to heavy load:
  • 1 Power Clean
  • 1 Front Squat
  • 1 Jerk

Goal: Smooth bar path, vertical drive.

Target: 70–80% of Clean & Jerk 1RM (185–210 lbs).

Rest: 90 seconds between sets.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Steam Engine” — For Time (18 min cap):
  • 21-15-9:
  • Power Cleans (135/95)
  • Bar-Facing Burpees
  • After each round: 250 m Echo Row

Stimulus: Power + Pace — fast cycle time on cleans, quick off the floor on burpees. Rows are your “reset.”

Goal: Finish under 14 minutes. Top athletes sub-10.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×10 Romanian Deadlifts (185/125)
  • 3×20 Banded Glute Bridges
  • 4×15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 3×1:00 Plank Holds
  • 10 min Easy Bike Cooldown

Intent: Reinforce hamstring and midline stability after heavy barbell work.
