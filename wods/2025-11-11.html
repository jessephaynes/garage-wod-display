Today's WOD — Steel Press

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Echo Bike
  • 10 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 10 Push-Ups to Down Dog
  • 10 Dumbbell Floor Press (light)
  • 5 Wall Walks or 20 sec Handstand Hold

Then: 2 rounds of 5 strict pull-ups + 5 strict dips or ring dips to build tension.

Part B — Strength

  • Bench Press: 5×5 @ 70–75% of 1RM
  • Superset with 8–10 Ring Rows (2-sec lower)

Focus: Drive barbell evenly, brace core tight.

Goal: Smooth control, even reps across all sets.

Part C — MetCon

  • 15-Minute AMRAP — "Push / Pull Drive"
  • 12 Dumbbell Push Press (50/35 lb)
  • 9 Toes-to-Bar
  • 6 Burpee Box Jump Overs (24/20")
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Shoulder + midline stamina under fatigue.

Goal: Maintain unbroken dumbbell sets; control breathing on the run.

Target: 4–5+ rounds.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4 Rounds (for quality):
  • 12 Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press
  • 15 Banded Face Pulls
  • 10 Barbell Bent-Over Rows (moderate)
  • 20 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 50 m Handstand Walk or 60 sec Wall Hold

Accessory Intent: Reinforce shoulder stability and upper back endurance.
