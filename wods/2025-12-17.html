Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 200 m Row
  • 10 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 10 Ring Rows
  • 8 Empty Bar Good Mornings
  • 8 PVC Pass-Throughs
  • Then, 2 Rounds:
  • 10 Jump Rope Singles
  • 5 Double Under Attempts
  • 5 Cal Ski (steady pace)

Intent: Prime posterior chain, shoulders, and aerobic system for long, cyclical work.

Part B — Strength

  • Weighted Pull-Up: Build to a 5-rep heavy over 5 working sets.
  • Rest 2:00 between sets.
  • Scaling: 5×5 Tempo Ring Rows (3-sec lower, 1-sec hold at top)

Focus: Midline tension and active shoulders throughout each rep.

Intent: Prep upper back and lats for rowing/ski work in Part C.

Part C — MetCon

  • 30-Minute EMOM
  • Minute 1: 12 Cal Row
  • Minute 2: 12 Cal Ski
  • Minute 3: 50 Double Unders

Stimulus: Aerobic capacity and breathing control across all 30 minutes.

Goal: Maintain 45–50 seconds of steady work per station; stay consistent across all rounds.

Pacing Tip: Row/Ski at 75–80% effort with relaxed grip to prevent fatigue.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 1. Shoulder Stability Superset (4 Rounds)
  • 10 Dumbbell Z-Press (50/35)
  • 15 Banded Face Pulls
  • 15 Banded External Rotations (each arm)
  • 2. Midline Endurance (3 Rounds)
  • 20 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 1:00 Plank Hold
  • 20 Russian Twists (50/35 DB)
  • 3. Aerobic Flush (Optional 20–30 min)
  • 5 Rounds:
  • 250 m Row (easy)
  • 250 m Ski (easy)
  • 250 m Run (easy)

Accessory Intent: Shoulder durability, posture reinforcement, and recovery flush after long EMOM.
