Part A — Warm-Up

  • 400m easy run
  • 2 rounds:
  • 5 empty-bar muscle cleans
  • 5 front squats
  • 5 push presses
  • 5 tall jerks
  • Build to workout weight

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:00 x 7 sets
  • 1 Clean Pull
  • 1 Hang Power Clean
  • 1 Squat Clean
  • 1 Jerk
  • Build from 135 lb toward 185–205 lb as technique allows

Focus: Efficient bar path, receiving position, and smooth transition into the jerk.

Intent: Prime speed and technical consistency without accumulating excessive fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5 Rounds For Time
  • 400m Run
  • 3 Clean & Jerks @ 155 lb

Stimulus: Moderate-weight barbell paired with sustainable but aggressive running.

Goal: Finish in 18–24 minutes while maintaining unbroken clean & jerks and consistent run splits.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 sets
  • 15 GHD hip extensions
  • 20 banded face pulls
  • 1:00 front rack stretch per side

Accessory Intent: Restore upper-back and posterior-chain function while improving front-rack mobility.
