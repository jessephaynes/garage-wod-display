Today's WOD — "Press the Issue"

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 300 m Row
  • 10 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 10 PVC Shoulder Pass-Throughs
  • 10 Dumbbell Strict Press (light)
  • 30-sec Hollow Hold
  • Then 2 Rounds of Handstand Prep:
  • 30-sec Wall-Facing Handstand Hold
  • 5 Wall Walks

Goal: Loosen shoulders, warm triceps and core, prep for handstand and pressing volume.

Part B — Strength

  • Strict Press: 5×5 @ 70% (≈145 lb)
  • Rest 90 sec between sets
  • Then Superset:
  • 3×8 Dumbbell Lateral Raises
  • 3×8 Strict Chin-Ups

Focus: Vertical press strength and shoulder stability under fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • 15-Minute AMRAP — "Shoulder Check"
  • 5 Handstand Push-Ups (or 10 DB Push Press 50/35)
  • 10 Toes-to-Bar
  • 15 Wall Balls (20/14 lb)
  • 10/8 Cal Ski Erg

Stimulus: Midline and shoulder endurance.

Goal: 5–6 rounds. Smooth, repeatable pace — unbroken TTB if possible.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds (Quality):
  • 12 GHD Back Extensions
  • 15 Ring Dips
  • 20 Russian Twists (50/35)
  • 400 m Easy Run
  • Then: 5 min banded shoulder mobility + 5 min thoracic spine foam roll

Accessory Intent: Strengthen pressing pattern and core control while promoting shoulder health.
