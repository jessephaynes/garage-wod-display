Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Row (build pace each round)
  • 100 m Easy Jog
  • 30 Single Unders
  • 10 Push-Ups
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 8 Scap Pull-Ups
  • Then:
  • 2 x 30 sec Double Under Practice
  • 2 x 60 m Progressive Strides

Part B — Strength

  • Bench Press: 5×5 @ 205 lbs
  • Optional Superset:
  • 12 Banded Face Pulls
  • 30-sec Hollow Hold

Focus: 2-second controlled descent, brief pause on chest, explosive drive up.

Intent: Build pressing strength and upper-body structure without taxing the engine for the MetCon.

Part C — MetCon

  • 8 Rounds For Time:
  • 15 Cal Row
  • 200 m Run
  • 50 Double Unders

Stimulus: Sustainable discomfort — lungs and calves under steady fatigue.

Goal: 18–24 minutes with consistent pacing across all 8 rounds.

Pacing: Row at ~85%, smooth relaxed run, break double unders before failure (30-20 or 25-25).

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×12 DB Bench Press (50s)
  • 4×12 Chest-Supported DB Rows (50s)
  • 3×15 Banded Tricep Pushdowns
  • 3–4 Rounds:
  • 20 Slow Calf Raises
  • 20 Tibialis Raises
  • 1 min Ankle Mobility
  • 20–30 min Zone 2 Echo Bike or Easy Jog (nasal breathing)

Accessory Intent: Upper-body hypertrophy, lower-leg durability, and aerobic recovery flush.
