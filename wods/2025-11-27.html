Today's WOD — Travel Fuel v2

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Bike or 200 m Run
  • 20 Single Unders → build to Double Unders
  • 10 Dumbbell Hang Snatches (light, alternating)
  • 10 Push-Ups
  • 10 Jumping Air Squats
  • 5 Burpees

Goal: Warm shoulders, elevate heart rate, and prime the posterior chain.

Part B — Strength Primer

  • Every 2:00 × 5 Rounds:
  • 6 Dumbbell Hang Snatches (Right)
  • 6 Dumbbell Hang Snatches (Left)
  • 4 Dumbbell Push Press (2×50/35 lb)

Focus: Crisp hip extension and smooth lockout.

Intent: Build quality movement and prep for the MetCon.

Part C — MetCon “Travel Fuel v2”

  • 4 Rounds for Time:
  • 50 Double Unders
  • 15 Dumbbell Hang Snatches (alt arms, 50/35 lb)
  • 12 Dumbbell Front Squats (2×50/35 lb)
  • 9 Burpee Over Dumbbells
  • 200 m Run or 0.3 mi Bike

Time Cap: 20 minutes

Stimulus: Moderate dumbbell loading with a steady breathing grind.

Goal: 15–17 minutes; stay unbroken on snatches, control transitions, and push through the burpees.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds (not for time):
  • 12 Bulgarian Split Squats (each leg, 1 DB goblet)
  • 15 Dumbbell Floor Press
  • 30 Russian Twists (bodyweight or 1 DB)
  • 1:00 Forearm Plank

Intent: Core and stability work to finish — keep movements controlled.
