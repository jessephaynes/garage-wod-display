Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds
  • 250m Row
  • 10 band pull-aparts
  • 10 scap pull-ups
  • 10 push-ups
  • 10 PVC pass-throughs or banded shoulder dislocates
  • 5 strict chest-to-bar attempts

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:00 x 8 sets
  • 1 Squat Snatch
  • 1 Hang Snatch
  • 1 Overhead Squat

Focus: Smooth barbell cycling, receiving position confidence, and overhead stability.

Intent: Build from moderate to moderately heavy loads while maintaining perfect positions and no missed lifts.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5 rounds for time
  • 500m Row
  • 8 Chest-to-Bar Pull-Ups
  • 10 Dumbbell Push Press (50 lb)
  • 12/10 Cal SkiErg
  • Time Cap: 22 min

Stimulus: Sustained aerobic effort with accumulating upper-body fatigue.

Goal: Finish in 16–20 minutes while maintaining consistent pacing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4 rounds
  • 10 Ring Rows with 3-second negative
  • 12 Dumbbell Incline Bench Press (35 lb)
  • 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 1:00 Dead Hang

Accessory Intent: Build pulling durability, shoulder health, and additional pressing volume.
