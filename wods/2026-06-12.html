Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds:
  • 250m Row
  • 10 Empty Bar Muscle Cleans
  • 8 Front Squats
  • 6 Push Press
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 20 Single-Unders
  • Then build:
  • 5 Clean & Jerks @ 95 lb
  • 3 Clean & Jerks @ 115 lb
  • 2 Clean & Jerks @ 135 lb

Part B — Strength

  • Every 90 seconds x 8 sets
  • 2 Hang Power Cleans
  • 1 Push Jerk
  • Build from 135 lb to a moderately heavy crisp weight

Focus: Fast turnover and aggressive lockout.

Intent: Prime the nervous system without accumulating fatigue before the conditioning piece.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5 Rounds for Time
  • 20/15 Cal Echo Bike
  • 8 Clean & Jerks (155 lb)
  • 500/400m Row
  • 8 Clean & Jerks (155 lb)
  • 25 minute cap

Stimulus: Sustainable engine with repeated moderate-heavy barbell exposure.

Goal: Finish in 18–24 minutes while keeping the clean and jerks mostly unbroken.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 rounds
  • 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 15 Strict Ring Rows
  • 1:00 SkiErg easy recovery pace

Accessory Intent: Build posterior-chain durability and upper-back volume while promoting recovery.
