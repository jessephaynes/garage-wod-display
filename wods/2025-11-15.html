Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Echo Row
  • 10 Good Mornings (empty barbell)
  • 10 Ring Rows
  • 10 Alternating Dumbbell Deadlifts (50/35)
  • 10 Hip Swings or Band Pull-Throughs

Intent: Prep hamstrings, lats, and pulling mechanics for today’s Olympic lift.

Part B — Strength

  • Power Clean + Hang Clean Complex: 5 sets (1 Power Clean + 2 Hang Cleans)
  • Build each set to ~75–80% of 1RM Clean & Jerk (≈ 200–210 lbs)
  • Rest 2–3 minutes between sets

Focus: Crisp turnover, maintain hook grip, and catch with active elbows.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Full Pull” — For Time (16-min cap):
  • 30 Power Cleans (135/95)
  • 40 Echo Bike Calories
  • 50 Wall Balls (20/14)
  • 40 Echo Bike Calories
  • 30 Power Cleans (115/75)

Stimulus: High power output early, controlled breathing through the bike, and grip management for cleans.

Goal: 13–15 minutes.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 10 Barbell Romanian Deadlifts (135/95)
  • 20 Banded Face Pulls
  • 10 Sandbag Over-Shoulders (100/150)

Accessory Intent: Posterior chain hypertrophy and upper back balance.
