Today's WOD

Open 26.3

Workout

  • For Time (16-Min Cap):
  • 2 Rounds:
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Cleans (95 lb)
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Thrusters (95 lb)
  • 2 Rounds:
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Cleans (115 lb)
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Thrusters (115 lb)
  • 2 Rounds:
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Cleans (135 lb)
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Thrusters (135 lb)

Stimulus: High-intensity Open-style grind. Manage fatigue early to survive the heavier bars.

Pacing: Smooth, sustainable pace on burpees. Aim to keep cleans in quick singles or small touch-and-go sets. Thrusters will be the separator—stay composed.

Goal: Finish under cap or push deep into final weight. Consistency beats early sprint.

Strategy Notes

  • Burpees: Stay low and efficient—step or hop with control
  • Cleans: Quick singles = smart play past round 1
  • Thrusters: Break early before failure (6-6 or 4-4-4)
  • Transitions: Move with urgency—no wasted time
