Today's WOD

Open 26.3

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 200 m Row
  • 8 Barbell Deadlifts (empty bar)
  • 6 Hang Power Cleans
  • 6 Front Squats
  • 6 Push Press
  • 4 Thrusters
  • Then:
  • 5 Burpees Over Bar
  • 5 Cleans (95 lb)
  • 5 Thrusters (95 lb)

Part B — Primer

  • Build Through:
  • 3 Cleans @ 95 / 115 / 135
  • 3 Thrusters @ each weight
  • Then:
  • 1 Round @ 115 lb:
  • 6 Burpees Over Bar
  • 6 Cleans
  • 6 Thrusters

Focus: Smooth cycling and fast transitions.

Intent: Feel each weight before the workout begins.

Part C — MetCon

  • For Time (16-Min Cap):
  • 2 Rounds:
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Cleans (95 lb)
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Thrusters (95 lb)
  • 2 Rounds:
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Cleans (115 lb)
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Thrusters (115 lb)
  • 2 Rounds:
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Cleans (135 lb)
  • 12 Burpees Over Bar
  • 12 Thrusters (135 lb)

Stimulus: High-intensity Open grind. Barbell cycling under fatigue.

Pacing: Smooth on burpees. Cleans into quick singles. Break thrusters early.

Goal: Stay consistent and push deep into final weight.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds (Not for Time):
  • 15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 12 Dumbbell Step-Ups (50/35)
  • 12 Banded Tricep Pushdowns
  • 10–15 Cal Echo Bike

Accessory Intent: Flush legs, midline, and light recovery work.
