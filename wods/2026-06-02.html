Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds: 250m Row, 10 Air Squats (3-sec descent), 8 PVC Pass-Throughs, 8 Front Rack Elbow Raises, 6 Paused Goblet Squats (2-sec pause)
  • Then 2 Rounds: 5 Empty Bar Front Squats, 5 Empty Bar Push Press, 5 Empty Bar Front Squats

Part B — Strength

  • Front Squat: 5 reps @ 185 lb
  • Front Squat: 5 reps @ 225 lb
  • Front Squat: 5 reps @ 245 lb
  • Then 4 x 3 Front Squat @ 270 lb with 3-second descent
  • Rest 2–3 minutes between working sets

Focus: Stay upright, keep elbows high, and own the bottom position.

Intent: Build quality front squat volume and confidence without testing a max.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5 Rounds For Time:
  • 12/10 Cal Echo Bike
  • 8 Front Squats @ 155 lb
  • 8 Pull-Ups
  • 100 ft Sandbag Carry (100 lb)

Stimulus: Moderate leg fatigue paired with sustained aerobic output.

Goal: Finish in 12–16 minutes while keeping all front squats unbroken.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds: 12 GHD Hip Extensions, 20 Weighted Sit-Ups (25 lb), 45-sec Front Rack Hold @ 225 lb, 1 min Couch Stretch per leg

Accessory Intent: Improve trunk strength and front rack endurance to support heavier front squats.
