Today's WOD — November 2, 2025

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Echo Row
  • 10 Scap Push-Ups
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs
  • 10 Dumbbell Strict Press (light)
  • 10 Jumping Lunges
  • 20-sec Forearm Plank Hold

Focus: Shoulder mobility + dynamic hip movement.

Part B — Strength

  • Strict Press + Push Press Complex
  • Every 2:00 × 6 sets:
  • 2 Strict Press + 3 Push Press
  • Build each round to a tough but smooth set (≈65–80% of 1RM Strict Press)

Focus: Maintain tight core; avoid overextending your back. Use legs only for push press.

Intent: Develop shoulder strength, stability, and lockout power.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Shoulder Storm” — 15-Minute AMRAP:
  • 10 Dumbbell Push Press (50/35s)
  • 10 Box Jump Overs (24/20”)
  • 200 m Run
  • 15 V-Ups
  • 10 Hand-Release Push-Ups

Stimulus: Shoulder endurance + midline control under fatigue.

Goal: 4–6 rounds — move consistently and breathe through presses and core work.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3×12 Dumbbell Lateral Raises
  • 3×12 Dumbbell Front Raises
  • 3×12 Banded Face Pulls
  • 3×20 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 3×12 Dumbbell Bench Press (moderate)
  • 3×30-sec Hollow Rock

Intent: Shoulder health, hypertrophy, and core control.
