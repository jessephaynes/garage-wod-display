Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds:
  • 250m Echo Row
  • 10 PVC pass-throughs or banded shoulder openers
  • 8 inchworm + push-up
  • 10 alternating Cossack squats
  • 6 strict pull-ups or ring rows
  • 6 empty bar muscle cleans + front squats
  • Then:
  • 2 x 20 sec handstand hold on wall
  • 2 x 10 fast singles or 30 sec jump rope sprint

Part B — Strength

  • Every 3:00 x 6 sets:
  • 2 Front Squats + 1 Split Jerk
  • Build across sets.
  • Suggested loading:
  • Set 1: 185
  • Set 2: 205
  • Set 3: 225
  • Set 4: 235
  • Set 5: 245
  • Set 6: 255–265 if sharp

Focus: Clean receiving position, vertical dip/drive, and aggressive lockout.

Intent: Heavy but technically crisp work with no missed jerks.

Part C — MetCon

  • 4 Rounds For Time:
  • 20/16 Cal Echo Bike
  • 15 Toes-to-Bar
  • 12 Dumbbell Thrusters (2x50/35)
  • 200m Run

Stimulus: Breathing and midline fatigue with repeated leg demand.

Goal: Finish in 14–18 minutes with sustainable intensity across all four rounds.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 rounds:
  • 15 Chest-Supported DB Rows
  • 20 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 40 sec Hollow Hold
  • 1:00 SkiErg easy flush
  • Then 5–10 min shoulder/band mobility

Accessory Intent: Restore posture and trunk integrity after front rack and thruster volume.
