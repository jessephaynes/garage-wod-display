Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250m Echo Row
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs or Band Shoulder Openers
  • 8 Empty Bar Muscle Cleans
  • 8 Front Squats
  • 6 Push Press
  • 10 Alternating Spiderman Lunges
  • 20 Double-Unders or 40 Singles
  • Then:
  • 2 x 20 sec Handstand Hold (wall)
  • 2 x 5 Box Jump Rebounds

Part B — Strength

  • Every 3:00 x 6 Sets:
  • 2 Front Squats + 1 Split Jerk
  • Set 1: 185
  • Set 2: 205
  • Set 3: 225
  • Set 4: 235
  • Set 5: 245
  • Set 6: 255–265 if moving well

Focus: Strong upright positioning out of the hole and aggressive lockout overhead.

Intent: Heavy but crisp. No missed lifts unless taking a calculated final shot.

Part C — MetCon

  • 4 Rounds For Time:
  • 20/16 Cal Echo Bike
  • 15 Toes-to-Bar
  • 12 Dumbbell Thrusters (2x50/35)
  • 200m Run
  • 6 Sandbag Over-Shoulder (150/100)

Stimulus: High breathing demand with leg fatigue accumulating every round.

Goal: Advanced 14–16 min. Intermediate 16–18 min. Bike at controlled intensity and stay composed on thrusters.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Sets:
  • 15 Ring Rows (tempo 31X1)
  • 12 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 20 Band Pull-Aparts
  • 1:00 Front Rack Carry (double DBs)
  • Then accumulate 3:00 Hollow Hold

Accessory Intent: Restore posture after front rack volume while reinforcing trunk stability and pulling endurance.
