Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250m Ski Erg
  • 10 Alternating Spiderman Lunges
  • 10 Banded Lat Pull-Downs
  • 8 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 10 Hollow Rocks
  • Then: 2 x (5 Beat Swings + 5 Kip Swings)

Part B — Strength

  • Push Press: 5×5 @ 155–175 lbs

Focus: Strong dip and vertical drive, finish stacked overhead.

Intent: Build upper body power without re-bending the knees.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5 Rounds For Time:
  • 13 Cal Ski
  • 14 Burpee to Target
  • 12–15 Toes-to-Bar
  • Rest 1:00 between rounds

Stimulus: Repeatable engine with core fatigue.

Goal: Maintain consistent pacing across all rounds.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds: 15 GHD Hip Extensions, 20 Weighted Sit-Ups, 30s Side Plank/side
  • 4 Sets: 12 DB Bench Press, 10 Ring Rows (tempo), 12 Banded Tricep Pushdowns
  • 10–15 min Easy Bike/Row/Ski (nasal breathing)

Accessory Intent: Build midline strength, upper body endurance, and promote recovery.
