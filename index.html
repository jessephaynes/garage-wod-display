Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250m Row
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs or Band Shoulder Openers
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 8 Push Press (empty bar)
  • 6 Strict Pull-Ups or Ring Rows
  • 20 Single-Unders
  • Then:
  • 2 x 20 sec Front Rack Stretch
  • 2 x 20 sec Thoracic Opener on Bench

Part B — Strength

  • Every 3:00 x 6 Sets:
  • 3 Front Squats
  • 5 Push Press
  • Build across all 6 sets.
  • Suggested Loading:
  • 155 / 175 / 185 / 195 / 205 / 215-225 lb

Focus: Fast elbows through the squat and aggressive vertical dip/drive on the press.

Intent: Heavy but crisp barbell cycling under fatigue preparation.

Part C — MetCon

  • "Engine Room"
  • 4 Rounds For Time:
  • 400m Run
  • 15 Toes-to-Bar
  • 12 Dumbbell Thrusters (50/35 lb)
  • 15/12 Calorie Echo Bike
  • Time Cap: 16 Minutes

Stimulus: High-output conditioning with repeatable rounds. Legs and lungs should progressively tighten each round.

Goal: Advanced athletes should aim for sub-13 minutes. Intermediate athletes should target finishing under the cap.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Sets:
  • 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 20 Banded Face Pulls
  • 1:00 Front Rack Carry (sandbag or double DBs)
  • 20 Hollow Rocks
  • Rest 90 seconds between sets.

Accessory Intent: Build trunk stability and upper-back durability to support front rack strength and cyclical pulling volume.
