Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds: 250m Row, 10 pass-throughs/band shoulder openers, 8 empty-bar front squats, 8 box step-ups (24"), 5 strict pull-ups or ring rows.
  • Then 2 rounds: 5 front squats @ 95 lb, 3 push jerks @ 95 lb, 2 hang power cleans @ 95 lb.

Part B — Strength

  • Front Squat: 5 @ 185 lb, 5 @ 205 lb, 4 @ 225 lb, 3 @ 255 lb, 3 @ 275 lb.
  • If 275 moves exceptionally well, perform a second set of 3 at 275 lb instead of increasing weight.
  • Optional: Front Rack Hold @ 275 lb for 20–30 seconds.

Focus: Aggressive elbows, strong brace, and a vertical torso through the sticking point.

Intent: Accumulate quality strength work without excessive fatigue and finish feeling powerful rather than depleted.

Part C — MetCon

  • 5 Rounds For Time: 12 Cal Echo Bike, 9 Front Squats (135 lb), 6 Bar-Facing Burpees, 3 Muscle-Ups.
  • Substitutions: 6 Chest-to-Bar Pull-Ups or 9 Pull-Ups.

Stimulus: Fast-paced conditioning where the bike elevates heart rate, front squats challenge recovery, and gymnastics demand composure under fatigue.

Goal: Advanced 12–15 min, Intermediate 15–18 min. Keep front squats unbroken and maintain consistent round times.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4 sets: 12 GHD Hip Extensions, 15 GHD Sit-Ups, 30 sec Sandbag Bear Hug Hold (150/100 lb), rest 90 sec.
  • Accumulate: 3 min Hollow Hold and 3 min Side Plank total.

Accessory Intent: Develop trunk stiffness, front-rack support, and posterior-chain endurance to support future squatting and Olympic lifting.
