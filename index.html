Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds for Quality:
  • 250m Echo Row
  • 10 PVC Good Mornings
  • 8 Inchworms with Push-Up
  • 10 Empty Bar Romanian Deadlifts
  • 8 Jump Squats

Goal: Elevate heart rate and prime hamstrings, glutes, and posterior chain for barbell work.

Part B — Strength

  • Deadlift: 5×3 @ 80% (≈ 290 lbs)
  • Rest 2:00 between sets
  • Then: 3×5 Snatch Grip Romanian Deadlift @ 50%

Intent: Reinforce posterior tension and setup consistency under load.

Part C — MetCon

  • 16-Minute AMRAP — “Chain Reaction”
  • 12 Cal Echo Bike
  • 9 Deadlifts (225/155)
  • 6 Burpee Box Jump Overs (24/20)
  • 3 Sandbag Over-the-Shoulder (100/80)

Stimulus: Moderate load, high output.

Goal: 4–5 rounds. Maintain steady breathing, unbroken deadlifts, and methodical transitions.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3×12 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 4×20 Banded Hamstring Curls
  • 3×15 Dumbbell Bench Press (50/35)
  • 3×15 Weighted Plank Pull-Throughs
  • 10 Minutes Easy Row Cooldown

Accessory Intent: Build resilient posterior chain and midline while improving recovery through light cyclical movement.
