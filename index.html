Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds (steady flow):
  • 200 m Row
  • 10 Good Mornings (empty barbell)
  • 10 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 8 Back Rack Lunges (45/35)
  • 10 Scap Pull-Ups
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs

Part B — Strength

  • Deadlift: 5×3 @ 80% of 1RM
  • Then: 2×5 Tempo Deadlifts (3-sec eccentric @ 60%)

Focus: Tight midline, vertical shins, bar close to body.

Goal: Build raw posterior strength with controlled tension.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Heavy Engine”
  • 15-Minute AMRAP:
  • 12 Dumbbell Deadlifts (50s/35s)
  • 9 Handstand Push-Ups (or DB Strict Press)
  • 6 Sandbag Over-Shoulders (100/80 lb)
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Steady grind. Move consistently through transitions.

Goal: 4–5 rounds — don’t redline early; breathe on the run.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×10 Barbell Hip Thrusts (moderate load)
  • 3×12 Bulgarian Split Squats (each leg)
  • 3×20 Banded Hamstring Curls
  • 3×15 GHD Back Extensions
  • Finisher: 3×200 m Farmer Carry (50s/35s)

Accessory Intent: Posterior durability + midline strength.
