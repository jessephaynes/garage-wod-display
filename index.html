Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 rounds, smooth pace:
  • 250m Echo Row
  • 8 PVC pass-throughs
  • 6 Tall Muscle Cleans
  • 6 Front Rack Reverse Lunges
  • 6 Push Press + 3 Push Jerks (light barbell)
  • 8 Box Step-Ups
  • 20 sec Front Rack Stretch
  • Then 2 rounds:
  • 3 High Hang Power Cleans
  • 3 Hang Squat Cleans
  • 3 Front Squats
  • 3 Split Jerks

Part B — Strength

  • Every 2:30 x 6 sets:
  • 1 Power Clean
  • 1 Hang Squat Clean
  • 2 Front Squats
  • 1 Split Jerk
  • Build across all 6 sets
  • Suggested loading: 135 → 155 → 175 → 195 → 205-215 → heavy technical finish

Focus: Stay smooth through the clean sequence and maintain strong front rack positioning before the jerk.

Intent: Develop clean receiving confidence, front rack stamina, and overhead stability under fatigue.

Part C — MetCon

  • 4 Rounds for Time:
  • 500/400m SkiErg
  • 15 Front Squats (135/95)
  • 12 Box Jump Overs (24/20)
  • 9 Toes-to-Bar

Stimulus: High-output engine piece with accumulating anterior-chain fatigue and sustained breathing pressure.

Goal: Finish in 14–18 minutes while keeping front squats controlled and movement quality consistent.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 rounds:
  • 1:00 Heavy Front Rack Carry
  • 15 GHD Hip Extensions
  • 12 Single-Arm DB Strict Press/side (35s)
  • 20 Hollow Rocks
  • Rest 1:00 between rounds

Accessory Intent: Build positional durability for Olympic lifting and improve trunk stability for front-loaded barbell work.
