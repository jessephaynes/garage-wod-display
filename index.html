Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Row
  • 10 Alternating Reverse Lunges
  • 8 Hang Power Cleans (empty barbell)
  • 8 Push-Ups
  • 6 Broad Jumps
  • Then: 2×10 Deadlift warm-up reps (building to 60–70% of today’s working weight)

Part B — Strength

  • Deadlift: Build to a heavy triple (3RM)
  • Then perform 2×3 @ 90% of top triple

Focus: Tight brace, smooth bar path, controlled lowering.

Intent: Heavy pull day with quality reps.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Pull the Pin” — 16-Minute AMRAP:
  • 12 Deadlifts (185/135)
  • 9 Handstand Push-Ups
  • 6 Bar-Facing Burpees
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Moderate weight, high turnover. Don’t redline early.

Goal: 5+ rounds, keep each round under 3 minutes.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×10 Romanian Deadlifts (DB or barbell @ moderate load)
  • 3×20 Hip Bridges (weighted optional)
  • 3×15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 3×1:00 Hollow Hold
  • Optional: 10 min easy Echo Bike flush

Accessory Intent: Posterior chain and core strength.
