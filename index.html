Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds, gradually increasing pace:
  • 10 Cal Echo Bike
  • 10 Good Mornings (PVC or empty bar)
  • 8 KB or Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts (light)
  • 10 Glute Bridges
  • 10 Sit-Ups
  • 5 Burpees
  • Then:
  • 2×10 Empty Bar Deadlifts
  • 2×10 Kip Swings or Hollow Rocks

Part B — Strength

  • Deadlift: 5×5 @ 70–75%

Focus: Controlled touch-and-go reps, flat back, full hip lockout.

Intent: Posterior chain volume and strength.

Part C — MetCon

  • 18-Minute AMRAP — "The Engine Room":
  • 500 m Echo Row
  • 12 Deadlifts (225/155)
  • 9 Toes-to-Bar (or V-Ups)
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Moderate-heavy barbell, sustainable aerobic effort.

Goal: 4–5 rounds with consistent transitions.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3×15 Hip Thrusts (pause 2 sec at top)
  • 4×12 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 3×15 Single-Leg Dumbbell RDLs (50/35)
  • 3×15 Weighted Back Extensions or Banded Good Mornings
  • Optional Finisher:
  • 3 Rounds:
  • 15 Cal Echo Bike
  • 10 Sandbag Over-Shoulder (100/80)
  • Rest 1 min between rounds

Intent: Strengthen glutes, hamstrings, and core while maintaining blood flow and recovery.
