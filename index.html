Today's WOD

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 250 m Echo Row
  • 10 Jumping Air Squats
  • 10 PVC Pass-Throughs
  • 5 Scap Pull-Ups + 5 Kip Swings
  • 10 Empty Barbell Good Mornings

Focus: Get the hips, shoulders, and posterior chain prepped for strength and skill work.

Part B — Strength / Skill

  • EMOM 12:
  • Minute 1: 6–8 Strict Pull-Ups (banded as needed)
  • Minute 2: 8–10 Dumbbell Front Rack Reverse Lunges (50/35s)
  • Minute 3: 10–12 GHD Sit-Ups (controlled — no bounce)

Intent: Bodyweight strength and midline activation under moderate fatigue.

Focus: Pull-ups: Active shoulders, full extension. Lunges: Balance & posture. GHDs: Controlled range — no overextension.

Part C — MetCon

  • 16-Minute AMRAP — “Flow State”:
  • 10 Pull-Ups
  • 20 Wall Balls (20/14)
  • 30 Double Unders
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Aerobic and rhythmic — keep moving for the full 16.

Goal: 4–6 rounds. Focus on smooth transitions and controlled breathing.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 4×10 Ring Rows (pause at top)
  • 3×20 Banded Good Mornings
  • 3×12 Dumbbell Lateral Raises (35/20)
  • 3×0:30 Hollow Hold + 0:30 Arch Hold

Accessory Intent: Reinforce midline and shoulder endurance to complement today's gymnastics work.
