Today's WOD — Oct 28, 2025

Part A — Warm-Up

  • 3 Rounds for quality:
  • 250 m Echo Row
  • 10 Good Mornings (empty barbell)
  • 10 Jumping Squats
  • 8 Strict Pull-Ups or Ring Rows
  • 8 Front-Rack Lunges (empty barbell)
  • 10 GHD Sit-Ups

Goal: Get hips, quads, and core warm — prepping for squatting and unilateral work.

Part B — Strength

  • Front-Rack Tempo Squat + Split Squat Combo
  • Every 3:00 × 5 sets:
  • 2 Tempo Front Squats (3-sec down, fast up) @ 205–215 lb
  • 6 Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squats per leg (50/35s)
  • Then: 3×10 Barbell Hip Thrusts @ 225 lb

Focus: Controlled descent, upright torso, and leg drive.

Intent: Quad and glute strength under tension; build positional strength and balance.

Part C — MetCon

  • “Brick by Brick” — Updated
  • 4 Rounds for Time (18 min cap):
  • 20 Thrusters (95/65)
  • 15 Dumbbell Snatches (50/35)
  • 12 Burpee Box Jump Overs (24/20)
  • 200 m Run

Stimulus: Conditioning + leg burn. You’ll move steady, recover on the run.

Goal: 14–17 minutes. Break thrusters early (10/10), then stay smooth on transitions.

Part D — Accessory (Optional)

  • 3 Rounds (not for time):
  • 15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 12 Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows (50/35)
  • 10 Sandbag Over-Shoulders (100/80)
  • 1:00 Hollow Hold

Intent: Core endurance and unilateral leg strength to complement today’s squatting volume.

Finish with 10–15 min mobility — hip flexors, quads, and shoulders.
