May 24, 2026 – Front Rack Engine

Front Rack Engine

Sunday, May 24, 2026

Part A — Warm-Up (6–8 min)

3 Rounds:

  • 250m Row
  • 8 PVC pass-throughs or banded shoulder openers
  • 10 Walking Lunges
  • 8 Empty Bar Front Squats
  • 6 Push Press
  • 4 Burpees

Then:

  • 2 rounds:
  • 5 Tall Cleans
  • 5 Front Squats
  • 5 Push Jerks

Focus on smooth front rack positioning and crisp footwork.

Part B — Strength (18 min)

Every 3:00 x 6 sets:

  • 2 Front Squats
  • 1 Split Jerk

Suggested Loading:

  • Sets 1–2: 185–205 lbs
  • Sets 3–4: 215–235 lbs
  • Sets 5–6: 245–275 lbs

Goal is strong bracing out of the hole and aggressive lockout overhead.

Part C — MetCon (16 min)

4 Rounds For Time

  • 500/450m Echo Bike
  • 12 Front Rack Walking Lunges (95/65 lbs)
  • 9 Toes-to-Bar
  • 6 Push Jerks (155/105 lbs)

Time Cap: 16 minutes

Stimulus: Moderately heavy barbell cycling under fatigue with respiratory pressure from the bike.

Pacing:

  • Bike: hard but sustainable
  • Lunges: unbroken if possible
  • Toes-to-bar: quick efficient sets
  • Jerks: smooth singles or fast touch-and-go

Target Finish:

  • Advanced: 11–13 min
  • Intermediate: 13–16 min

Part D — Optional Accessory Work (60–90 min)

Upper Back / Shoulder Stability

  • 3 Sets:
  • 15 Band Face Pulls
  • 12 DB Rear Delt Raises
  • 30-sec Ring Support Hold

Midline Conditioning

  • 3 Rounds:
  • 15 GHD Sit-Ups
  • 20 Hip Extensions
  • 1:00 Front Plank

Aerobic Flush

  • 20 min easy Echo Bike
  • 20 min conversational run
  • 5k easy row

Mobility

  • Front rack stretching
  • Thoracic extension work
  • Couch stretch
  • Lat band distraction
